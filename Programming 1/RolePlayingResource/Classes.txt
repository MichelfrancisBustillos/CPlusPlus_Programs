Class:

Barbarian (Bbn) - archetypal warrior who uses brute strength and raw fury to excel in combat, instead of the honed skills

Bard (Brd) - use their artistic talents to induce magical effects

Cleric (Clr) - skilled in the use of divine magic. Clerics are powerful healers due to the large number of healing and 	curative magics available to them. With divinely-granted abilities over life or death, they are also able to repel or control undead creatures.

Druid (Drd) - 

Fighter (Ftr) - a versatile, weapons-oriented warrior who fights using skill, strategy and tactics.

Monk (Mnk) - martial artist, specializing in unarmed combat.

Paladin (Pal) - a holy knight, crusading in the name of good and order, and is a divine spellcaster. 

Ranger (Rgr) -  hunters, skilled woodsmen, and often live reclusive lives as hermits.

Rogue (Rog)  - a versatile character, capable of sneaky combat and nimble tricks. 

Sorcerer (Sor) - weak in melee combat, but a master of arcane magic, generally the most powerful form of magic. Sorcerers' magical ability is innate rather than studied.

Wizard (Wiz) - uses arcane magic, and is considered less effective in melee combat than other classes.
